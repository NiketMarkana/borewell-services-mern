# ✨ Borewell Services: Full Functionality Overview ✨

Welcome to the comprehensive guide of features and capabilities for the **Borewell Services & Pipes** MERN application.

---

## 🔐 User Authentication & Roles
> [!NOTE]
> The system uses JWT (JSON Web Tokens) for secure, role-based access control.

*   **Registration & Login:** Secure user onboarding with password hashing (bcrypt).
*   **Role: User:** Can browse, place orders, and track their own history.
*   **Role: Employee:** Can view all orders and update statuses.
*   **Role: Admin:** Full system control, including employee management and order overrides.

---

## 🚰 Borewell Services
**Seamlessly request professional borewell drilling and maintenance.**

*   **Detailed Request Form:** Submit name, contact, date, and precise location details.
*   **Technical Specifications:** Input expected depth and any additional site requirements.
*   **Tracking:** Real-time visibility into the request status (Pending → Approved → In Process → Completed).

---

## 🛒 Pipe & Motor Products
**Browse and order high-quality irrigation and plumbing hardware.**

*   **Categorized Catalog:**
    *   🔵 **HDPE Pipes:** Sprinkler, Coil, and Black pipes.
    *   🟢 **PVC Pipes:** Agricultural, UPVC, and various inch sizes.
    *   ⚙️ **Submersible Motors:** 1HP and 1.5HP reliable pump sets.
*   **Flexible Ordering:** Specify quantity, unit (meter/feet), and custom budget requirements.
*   **Order Workflow:** Track product journey (Processing → Shipped → Delivered).

---

## 📊 Order Tracking & Management (User)
*   **Personal Dashboard:** View all past and present service/product orders.
*   **Status History:** Clickable timeline showing exactly when a status was updated.
*   **Color-Coded Badges:** Quick visual cues for order status (Green = Success, Red = Cancelled, Yellow = Pending).

---

## 🛠️ Admin & Employee Dashboard
*   **Centralized Management:** View all customer requests in one unified interface.
*   **Status Updates:** One-click status transitions with automatic history logging.
*   **Secure Access:** Protected routes ensuring only authorized staff can modify data.

---

## 💡 Maintenance & Guides
**A dedicated section to help users extend the life of their equipment.**

*   **Checklists:** Clear "Do's" and "Don'ts" for Borewells, Pipes, and Motors.
*   **Proactive Warnings:** "Call Service If" indicators for early problem detection.

---

## 📞 Contact & Support
*   **Company Information:** Easy access to phone, email, and physical address.
*   **Responsive Design:** Fully functional on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers.
